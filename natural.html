Getting Started

So you're thinking about going natural? Well, you've reached a great place for tips to get your started. If you don't know whether to big chop, cut out your relaxed hair, or just wear a protective style until you feel comfortable. Start with researching the web for ideas, watch YouTube videos, or get advice from your peers who are natural. Which ever way you decide to start just know your hair type/texture will not be like everyone else. Whatever, your hair type is embrace it and love it! Start small and get better with time. Set goals on how you want your hair to be and achieve them.

Styling Your Hair

There's different ways to style your hair when your natutral and that's the fun part. With the mutliple ways of protecting your hair by styling you start with one and eventually try them all. Below there is a list below of tips to start.

  • No chemicals/textrizures
  • The BIG chop.
  • Protective braids/twists.
  • Weave/Wigs.
  • Less/NO heat. To prevent heat damage.

    • Products To Use

    You've now done your research on natural hair and now is wondering what products you should start with. Advice when shopping for products. EXPENSIVE does not mean better! Look up products and get recommendations from your peers. Figure out what your hair type is so you can pick out the correct products. What one product does for others may not do the same for you and that's okay. It's a trial and error until you find the correct products for your hair type. Deep condition and moisturize your hair regularly. Protect your hair at night with satin pillows, bonnets, or hair scarfs.

    Love Your Natural

    Don't forget going natural isn't as easy as you think. It requires maintenance and patience. Just because your hair type isn't what you expected doesn't mean you give up. Always love yourself in your natural state. Embrace what you have and be proud you have it.
