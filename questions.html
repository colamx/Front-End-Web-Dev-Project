2.I wanted to know what my hair would be like without a relaxer. I've had a relaxer the majority of my life and i like to switch things up with my hair all the time.
3. i did the "Big Chop" in high school.
4. STG- keep my hair up consisitely while in a TWA(teeny weeny afro). LTG- grow my hair out for two years.
5.All natural porducts(coconut oil, olive oil, tea tree oil, jojoba oil, water, shea butter).
6.Dont focus on your hair type, healthy hair is all that matters! Do not let people touch your hair if they have no idea what they're doing. Your hair is sensitive and doesnt require everyones touch and input. Your natural hair can be figured out by a professional r you can do it on your own. The goal is to figure it out so you know how to manage your hair. Buy products in bulk either online or Sam's club. Healthy hair is healthier when you eat better and when you feel better.Get to know yourself, ratinalize the people aroundyou Embrace that beautiful rich complex goddess qualities us natruals have. Make this a life changing experrience from the inside out and your natural hair will be more than just a hair trend.
